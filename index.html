about us

what makes our coffee special?

I wanted to take this time to let you know how grateful we are to have you choosing us for your coffee needs. Quality coffee is a passion for me and I am so thankful for your patronage which allows me to do this. I wanted to share a little about our coffees, and why I think they are so special. First of all, we buy our coffee from only the most reputable farms and mills in Kona. Being a teacher in Kona for three decades, I taught some of the farmers and some of their parents too - which goes a long way.

There are so many brewing methods out there, it can seem like a daunting task to find the perfect one for you. We'd love to share some info on some of our tried and true favorites, though everyone's taste varies so we encourage you to experiment and find your own!

Papua New Guinea Moanti® by admin / 21st may, 2022

In Papua New Guinea, where the coffee business is led primarily by men, Moanti Ise is a one-woman force for enduring change. She single-handedly brought hundreds of smallholder farmers together, creating a consistent, high-quality supply chain that is improving lives and strengthening the entire community. Moanti has also transformed her community by advocating for a safe, clean water system. Through this critical project, local families now have essential access to water. We are in awe of Moanti’s leadership and accomplishments and honored to share this exceptional cup with notes of cocoa nib and thyme.
